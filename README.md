# PETYA CONSTRUCTED LANGUAGE DOCKET (CONLANG)

**Designation:** Rugged, ancient, mathematical, and martial conlang.
**System Theme:** Slate, Iron, Hearth, and Legacy.

---

## 1. PHONOLOGY (SOUND SYSTEM)
*   **The Rolled R:** Every 'r' in Petya must be heavily trilled/rolled (alveolar trill)[span_0](start_span)[span_0](end_span). Examples: *Vrak* is pronounced [Vr-r-rahk], *Skar* is pronounced [Skahr-r-r][span_1](start_span)[span_1](end_span). A prolonged final trill indicates emphasis or ceremonial weight[span_2](start_span)[span_2](end_span).
*   **The Throat Snap (Guttural Clusters):** Letter combinations starting with consonants like *Kt-* (e.g., *Ktag*) require a sharp, sudden air release at the back of the throat[span_3](start_span)[span_3](end_span).
*   **Final Sound Clipping:** Suffixes ending in *-ug*, *-ag*, or *-oft* must be clipped sharply[span_4](start_span)[span_4](end_span).

## 3. GRAMMAR RULES & STRUCTURES
*   **Rule 1 (Syntax):** Subject + Object + Verb (SOV). Action verb ends the sentence[span_52](start_span)[span_52](end_span)[span_53](start_span)[span_53](end_span).
*   **Rule 2 (Plural):** Append `-yek` to the end of the root[span_54](start_span)[span_54](end_span)[span_55](start_span)[span_55](end_span).
*   **Rule 3 (Past Tense):**
    *   *Common Dialect:* Shift vowel to "u" and append `-kd` or `-gd`[span_56](start_span)[span_56](end_span).
    *   *Formal Dialect:* Shift noun root vowel to "u" and append `-d` / `-td`[span_57](start_span)[span_57](end_span).
*   **Rule 4 (Material Prepend):** Prepend material root to structural nouns (e.g., *Fenikt-* + *Buktstrig*)[span_58](start_span)[span_58](end_span).
*   **Rule 5 (Articles):** Use *Blik* (consonants) or *Blikna* (vowels)[span_59](start_span)[span_59](end_span).
*   **Rule 6 (Connectors):** *Vist* (with), *Gyat* (to), *Likst* (like), *Trog* (but), *Nagcerkt* (if)[span_60](start_span)[span_60](end_span).
